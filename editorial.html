Behind the Camera

Many of these photoshoots are a product of collaboration with others. I worked with several models, creative directors, makeup artists, and stylists to help realize a collective vision by helping with the sets, overall direction, photography, and editing.

I am specifically passionate about capturing the feminine experience through the "female gaze," reclaiming what it means to be a photographer and celebrating girlhood + imaginative whimsy.
