rhythm, a zine

Inside the Zine

"Rhythm, the movement of life" is a zine that studies the meaning of the word, “rhythm” while exploring typographic methods and editorial design.

Storyboards and Sketches

I knew I wanted the zine to study the definition of the word through a narrative. I started brainstorming and. I found a pattern of rhythm being in every part of life, and I used this concept to spearhead the story.

First Iterations

After establishing the font choice and color combination, I played with my first iterations. I realized that I needed to shift into a balance of being artistic and being consistent with my layout.

Final Spread

This was my final design.. I added a table of contents and made design choices that contributed to the narrative, while staying true to the a 4-column, 3-row grid.
